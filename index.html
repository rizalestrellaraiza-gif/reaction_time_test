🎨 Neon vs Neutral Reaction Time Test

This test measures how quickly you can respond to different colors — neon (bright) and neutral (soft) tones. You’ll see colors appear one by one. Your task is simple:

Press the SPACEBAR the moment you see a color appear — as fast as you can!

There will be 12 rounds in total — 6 neon and 6 neutral colors shown in random order.
Some will appear faster or slower, so stay alert!

Are you ready?
